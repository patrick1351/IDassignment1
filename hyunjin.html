Hyunjin

Stage Name: Hyunjin (현진)
Birth name: Hwang Hyun Jin (황현진)
Brithday: March 20, 2000
Height: 179 cm (5’10.5″)

Facts

– He was born in Seoul, South Korea.
– He is an only child.
– He graduated from SOPA in February 2019, he studied Practical Dance.
– Hyunjin, D-CRUNCH‘s Chanyoung, and VERIVERY‘s Yongseung are SOPA classmates.

– Hyunjin’s English name is Sam Hwang.
