History

Stray Kids (Korean Hangul: 스트레이 키즈), shortened as SKZ, is a South Korean k-pop boy group formed by JYP Entertainment through the 2017 reality show of the same name, Stray Kids. The group officially debuted on the March 25 of 2018 with the album District 9 (I Am Not). 3 of the members are part of a rap/hip hop sub-unit called 3RACHA. 3RACHA composes the majority of Stray Kids' songs under the sub-unit name. Stray Kids is one of the unique groups as they created themselves, unlike all of JYPE's other groups who were created by the company itself.

Stray Kids has won 20 awards out of 41 nomination such as the Asia Artist Awards rookie of the year award, Soribada Best K-Music Awards Rising Hot Star Award and MTV Europe Music Awards for the best Korean Act.

Currently, Stray Kids has released a total of one studio album, two compilation albums, one reissue, eight extended plays, and fourteen singles.
